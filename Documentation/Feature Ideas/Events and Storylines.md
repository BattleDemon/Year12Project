## Storylines

#### Frankish Story
The main story of the game allows every franks culture realm to attempt a conquest of the French region, if the player isn't of frank culture it defaults to the Merovingians else they only have a buff

Once settled in France for 25 years a new culture will evolve the Frankish culture

It will then give the player a free war against either the Germanic region, Hispanic, England, or Roman regions, If the AI is playing it will select the historical option
